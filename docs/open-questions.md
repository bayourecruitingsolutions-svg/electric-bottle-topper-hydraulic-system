# Open Questions

## Process
- What type of bottles will be handled?
- What type of tops/caps will be handled?
- Is the process intended for one bottle size or multiple sizes?
- What cycle time is expected?
- What production rate is expected?

## Mechanical
- What spindle geometry is planned?
- How much rotation is required?
- How are the three smaller cylinders mechanically linked to the gripping head?
- What materials contact the cap and bottle?
- What changeover is needed between bottle sizes?

## Hydraulic
- What operating pressure range is available?
- What pressure is needed for the spindle drive?
- What pressure is needed for the gripping cylinders?
- What pressure reduction range is required to avoid bottle damage?

## Controls
- What PLC platform is preferred?
- What HMI platform is preferred?
- What signals are analog vs digital?
- Is recipe management required?

## Measurement
- What torque range must be measured?
- What load cell range is needed?
- How will torque be calculated from the mechanical layout?
- Is differential pressure needed only for display or also for control?

## Networking
- Is remote monitoring required in the first version?
- What information should be visible remotely?
- Should remote access be read-only?
- What camera views are needed?
