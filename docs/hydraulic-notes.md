# Hydraulic Notes

## Purpose
This document stores preliminary ideas related to the hydraulic design of the Electric Bottle Topper.

## Main Hydraulic Functions
- Power spindle rotation through a primary hydraulic cylinder
- Control bottle top gripping through three smaller hydraulic cylinders
- Regulate pressure to prevent damage to bottles and caps
- Support forward and reverse motion control
- Monitor system pressure and return pressure

## Major Hydraulic Elements
- Hydraulic pump
- Electric motor
- Reservoir
- Relief valve
- Manifold
- Directional control valves
- Pressure reducing valves
- Primary cylinder
- Three smaller grip cylinders
- Supply and return pressure sensing

## Design Considerations
- Pressure must be limited to avoid breaking glass bottles
- Motion should be smooth and controlled
- Return pressure can be used to understand system loading
- Manifold layout should simplify maintenance and plumbing
- Relief and reducing valves should provide mechanical protection independent of software

## Open Questions
- What operating pressure range is available?
- What cylinder bore/stroke sizes are needed?
- What pressure should be used for gripping vs spindle motion?
- Should the grip cylinders move together or independently?
- Is position feedback needed?
