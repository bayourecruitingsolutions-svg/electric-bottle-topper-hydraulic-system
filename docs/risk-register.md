# Risk Register

## Purpose
This document tracks early project risks so they can be discussed and managed as the project develops.

| ID | Risk | Impact | Likelihood | Notes / Possible Mitigation |
|----|------|--------|------------|-----------------------------|
| R1 | Bottle breakage due to excessive force or torque | High | Medium | Use pressure reducing valves, force limits, careful mechanical design |
| R2 | Incomplete client requirements | High | High | Continue collecting notes and clarifying open questions |
| R3 | Pressure/torque sensing may not match real process behavior | Medium | Medium | Plan for calibration and validation later |
| R4 | Scope creep from advanced remote features | High | High | Separate base system from future features |
| R5 | Safety requirements may be more complex than currently documented | High | Medium | Plan formal safety review before implementation |
| R6 | Mechanical alignment challenges could affect repeatability | High | Medium | Document mechanical assumptions early |
| R7 | Hydraulic pressure spikes could damage bottles or tooling | High | Medium | Include relief and pressure reducing protection |
| R8 | Hardware selection may change controls approach | Medium | Medium | Keep documentation hardware-agnostic for now |
| R9 | Remote monitoring / networking may introduce security concerns | Medium | Medium | Keep remote features limited in early concept stage |
| R10 | Torque calculation may require more than one sensing method | Medium | Medium | Compare pressure-based and load-cell-based methods later |

## Notes
This is an early planning risk list and should be updated as the project becomes more defined.
